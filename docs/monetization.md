# Monetization

than a generic monitoring tool.

- Family Pro
- School Laptop
- Coaching Center
- Developer Workstation
- Small Business Productivity
- Exam Mode

High-value features:

- Weekly AI report
- Browser Intelligence
- Device Health Score
- Risky Software Detection
- Tamper and Trust Center
- Delivery Assurance
- Cloud archive retention
- Role-based dashboards
- Policy templates

The buyer-facing product should always distinguish live evidence from demo proof.
